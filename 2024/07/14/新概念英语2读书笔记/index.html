如何造好一个句子？

句子要有主语、动词、而且动词要有变化才行。

以及要有形容词、副词、时间、地点等

L1. A private conversation

单词造句

  1. private

    This is my private life.

    This is a private conversation.

    His father takes him to a private school every day.

    Does his father take him to a private school every day?

    How does his father take him to a private school every day?

    Where does his father take him to every day?

    Why does his father take him to a private school every day?

  2. conversation

    I had an interesting conversation with him in the park yesterday.

    Did you have an interesting conversation with him in the park yesterday?

    How was your conversation with him in the park yesterday?

    We are having a conversation in the library. So, be quiet, please.

    Don’t have a conversation loudly as we are in the library.

    ( Please don’t talk loudly as we are in the library. 更常用，但是为了造句…)

  3. theatre

    I have never been to the theatre.

    Have you been to the theatre?

    • Yes, I went to the theatre seven months ago.
    • Yes, I have been to the theatre before.

    Why have you not been to the theatre?

    • I’m not really into theatre performances. ( not really into 对…不感兴趣 )

    How did you go to the theatre yesterday?

    • I took the subway with my best friend yesterday.

  4. seat

    I have a good seat.

    Why did you get a good seat?

    How did you get a good seat?

    Where did you find your good seat?

  5. play

    The play was very interesting.

    I am going to watch a play with my mum the day after tomorrow.

    How are you going to watch the play with your mum?

    How often do you watch a play?

  6. loudly

    Talk loudly

    We are talking too loudly.

    Why are you talking so loudly?

    It was too loud to work at the office yesterday.

    Was the office too loud yesterday?

  7. angry

    I had to get angry.

    You look very angry, I advise you to take it easy. ( look at 被看的东西或人)

    Why did you angry yesterday?

    When did you angry in the office?

    Can you talk about the reason for his angry? ( the angry reson 英语中没有这样的表达)

  8. angrily

    He looked at her angrily this afternoon.

    He spoke angrily to the customer who had complained.

    I tried to calm him down, but he just shouted angrily at me.

  9. attention

    pay attention to sb./sth. 注意某人某物

    You are not paying attention to me.

    Why are you not paying attention to him?

    When are you paying attention to him?

    What are you paying attention to about him?

  10. bear

    I can’t bear that.

    Can you bear that?

    How can you bear that?

  11. business

    It is none of your business.

  12. rudely

    Don’t be rude. Let him in.

    He was talking to her rudely.

    How was he talking to her?

    What rude things was he saying to her?

短语造句

  1. Have a conversation

  2. Take a seat

  3. Is the seat taken?

    Excuse me, is the seat taken? I didn’t see anyone sitting there.

  4. Pay attention

  5. How can you bear that?

  6. Don’t be rude.
